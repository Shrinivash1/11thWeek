NIELIT (National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology) is an autonomous scientific society under the administrative control of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India. It is engaged in both formal and non-formal education in the areas of Electronics and Information Technology. NIELIT plays a vital role in training, capacity building, and certification, and is recognized as a national examination body for accrediting institutions that conduct courses in IT and electronics. With a vision to be the leader in industry-oriented education, NIELIT continues to contribute to the development of skilled professionals in the field of Information, Electronics, and Communication Technology across India.
